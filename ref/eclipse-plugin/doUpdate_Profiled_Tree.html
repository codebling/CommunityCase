I have noticed that there are too many COM calls.. each isCheckedout, isHijacked etc.. call is
converted into jacob-com-call. But in fact we can just Cache the clearcase ICCVersion one time,
and ask the needed info from that object. My first rough calculation show that this would gives us
a 60% decrease of calls to the JNI-layer. 
Of course this would break the ClearTool vs JNI transparancy but that can be solved by supporting
two branches [win32|other].
